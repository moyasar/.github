# Moyasar

Moyasar is a payment infrastructure platform founded in 2015 and supervised by the Saudi Central Bank (SAMA). We connect businesses, banks, and institutions through a secure, integrated payments layer. 

## What We Do

- **Accept Payments** — Hosted checkout, payment links, in-app SDKs, and direct API integration supporting Mada, Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Apple Pay, Google Pay, Samsung Pay, and more.
- **Manage & Automate** — Multi-merchant management, settlements, split payments, and payout services.
- **Payment Gateway** — Dedicated gateway through Moyasar Payment Gateway (MPG).
- **Optimize** — Smart routing, advanced analytics, and sandbox testing.
